📊 Bank Customer Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)   

📌 Project Overview   
The Bank Customer Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze customer behavior, engagement, and churn patterns in the banking sector.
This dashboard helps identify customer retention trends, credit card usage, and exit patterns using data visualization and business intelligence techniques.
The goal of this project is to transform raw banking data into meaningful insights that support business decision-making.

🎯 Objectives    
Analyze total customers and active vs inactive members
Identify customer exit trends
Compare credit card holders vs non-credit card holders
Understand gender-wise customer distribution
Track customer activity over time

📈 Dashboard Insights

1️⃣ Customer Overview    
Total Customers: 10,000
Active Members: 5,151
Inactive Members: 4,849
Retained Customers: 7,963
Exited Customers: 2,037
This section provides a quick overview of customer engagement and retention.

2️⃣ Customer Activity Trend    
A bar chart visualizing Active Members vs Inactive Members by Year helps understand how customer activity changes over time.
Key Insight:
Active members show a gradual increase over the years.

3️⃣ Customer Exit Analysis    
A line chart tracks Exit Customers by Month and compares it with Previous Customer Exit.
Key Insight:
Some months show higher exit rates, indicating potential churn patterns.

4️⃣ Credit Card Holder Analysis    
A pie chart compares:
Credit Card Holders
Non-Credit Card Holders
Key Insight:
Around 70% of customers hold credit cards, showing strong adoption.

5️⃣ Gender-Based Analysis    
A donut chart shows Non-Credit Card Holders by Gender.
Key Insight:
Male customers have a slightly higher percentage of non-credit card usage compared to females.
